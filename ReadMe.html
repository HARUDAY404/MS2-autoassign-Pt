MS/MS Spectrum Analysis & Auto-Assignment

Mass spectrometry takes advantage of MS/MS spectra to provide sequence information of peptide. One has to, however, scrutinize each MS/MS spectrum when it comes to locate the residue thats been covalently labeled. The motivation of writing this app is to achieve automatic MS/MS spectrum assignments and annotations.
